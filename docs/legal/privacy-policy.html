By using the Site, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

1. Information We Collect

Information you give us

  • Contact form: your name, email address, country, an optional phone number, and the message you send us.
  • Newsletter: your email address and, optionally, your first name.
  • Support chat: the messages you send in our chat widget, and any contact details you choose to share in the conversation.
  • Scheduling a call: if you book a meeting, our scheduling provider (Calendly) collects your name, email, and booking details on our behalf.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage & device data: pages viewed, links clicked, referring pages, approximate location (from IP address), browser type, and device, collected via Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see "Cookies & Analytics" below.
  • Server logs: standard technical information (such as IP address and timestamps) kept for security and reliability.

We do not intentionally collect special categories of data (e.g., health, religion) or payment card details through the Site.

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide the services or information you request;

  • To understand how the Site is used and improve its content and performance;
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK users)

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on: consent (e.g., newsletter sign-up and non-essential cookies); legitimate interests (e.g., responding to enquiries, securing and improving the Site); and compliance with legal obligations. You may withdraw consent at any time.

We use cookies and similar technologies to run the Site and to measure usage through Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager. You can control cookies through your browser settings, and you can opt out of Google Analytics via Google's opt-out tools. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers ("sub-processors") who help us operate the Site and our business, under appropriate confidentiality and data-protection terms:

  • Cloudflare — domain, DNS, security, and email routing

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or in connection with a business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition).
